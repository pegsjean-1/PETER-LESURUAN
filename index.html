About Peter Lesuruan

Peter Lesuruan is a licensed professional land surveyor based in Maralal Town, Samburu County, Kenya. With over 15 years of experience in geomatics and land administration, he has established a reputation for precision, reliability, and professional integrity.

Specializing in cadastral surveys, engineering surveys, and GIS mapping, Peter serves a diverse clientele including individual landowners, real estate developers, construction companies, and government agencies across northern Kenya.

His commitment to utilizing modern surveying technologies ensures accurate, legally compliant survey data that stands up to regulatory scrutiny and protects your property rights.
