About Peter Lesuruan

Peter Lesuruan is a professional land surveyor based in Maralal Town Kenya with extensive experience in land surveying, mapping and geospatial data collection.

He provides accurate survey services for land owners, engineers, developers and government institutions using modern surveying technologies.

Survey Process

1. Site Visit

Surveyor inspects the land and collects initial information.

2. Field Data Collection

Using GPS and total stations to collect accurate measurements.

3. Data Processing

Survey data is processed using surveying software.

4. Survey Map Preparation

Survey plans and maps are produced.

5. Survey Report

Final documents are delivered to the client.
