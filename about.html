About Peter Lesuruan

Peter Lesuruan is a licensed land surveyor based in Maralal Town, Kenya. With **9 years of experience**, he specializes in boundary surveys, topographic surveys, land subdivision, and construction setting out.

Peter is committed to providing accurate, reliable, and legally compliant surveying services using GPS, total stations, and GIS technology. He has served numerous clients across Kenya, ensuring precision and professionalism in every project.

Qualifications & Affiliations

  • Licensed Land Surveyor – Survey of Kenya
  • Member – Institution of Surveyors of Kenya (ISK)
  • Certified in GPS Surveying & GIS Applications
  • Trained at Kenya Institute of Surveying and Mapping
